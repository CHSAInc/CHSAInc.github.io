VitalCheck Public Documentation

VitalCheck is an innovative AI-powered symptom checker designed to empower individuals to take control of their health. Our mission is to provide accessible, reliable, and personalized health information to help you make informed decisions about your well-being.

How VitalCheck Works

VitalCheck combines your self-reported symptoms with your medical history (if you choose to link your health records) to analyze potential health concerns. Our advanced AI algorithms, powered by Google's Gemini, evaluate this information and generate personalized insights and recommendations.

Data Usage and Privacy

Protecting your privacy is our top priority. We are committed to complying with all applicable privacy laws, including HIPAA, to safeguard your health information. We only collect and use data that is necessary to provide you with accurate and relevant health assessments.

Information We Collect:

  • **Self-Reported Symptoms:** We collect the symptoms you enter into the app, including their severity and duration.
  • **Medical History (Optional):** If you choose to link your electronic health records (EHRs), we may collect relevant information such as diagnoses, medications, and allergies to provide more personalized insights.

How We Use Your Data:

  • **Symptom Analysis:** We use your data to analyze your symptoms and identify potential health conditions.
  • **Personalized Recommendations:** We provide tailored recommendations based on your symptoms and medical history.
  • **Service Improvement:** We use de-identified, aggregated data to improve our algorithms and the overall functionality of VitalCheck.

How We Protect Your Data:

  • **Encryption:** We use strong encryption to protect your data in transit and at rest.
  • **De-identification:** We de-identify your health information before using it for analysis or service improvement.
  • **Access Controls:** We restrict access to your data to authorized personnel only.

API Integration (For Developers)

VitalCheck integrates with the following APIs:

  • Epic MyChart FHIR API
  • Google Gemini AI API

For more detailed technical information, please refer to our developer documentation.

Disclaimer

VitalCheck is not a substitute for professional medical advice. The information provided by the App is for informational purposes only and should not be used for self-diagnosis or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for any health concerns.
